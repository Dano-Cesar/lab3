About this site

As risdents of Washington, I believe we have a responsibility in protecting and saving the southern resident orcas. As they spend their springs, summers, and fall hunting for salmon along the coast of Washingtion and British Columbia. We must keep our oceans clean of garbage, keep dangerous chemicals out of the ocean to save thier food source.
